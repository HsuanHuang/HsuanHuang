Project Goal

This project was the final project of my Web Programming class. I collaborated with three students from different majors to form an inter-disciplinary team and build a website. Escape is a website which parsed through the data about exhibitions in Taiwan. Users could get the information about exhibitions held in every major museum, salon, and live house in one website.
